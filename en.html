[ About ]

The online advertising ecosystem is one of the main funding sources of the internet, allowing us to get access to multiple types of services for free, from Facebook to our favorite newspaper. This new way to deliver ads brought the capacity to target individuals based on their behavior. This behavior is transformed into "interests", which are associated with users. However, not all the interests may have a relation to our real behavior and could be that some of them are artificially added to increase the potential audience of the ad campaigns. In this project, we aim to analyze the number of interests associated with your Facebook and Google accounts. Also, we want to measure the level of over profiling (i.e., how many of those interests they assign you but do not represent you ) as well as to measure how frequently Facebook and Google use those interests to deliver ads. To this end, we need your help. We have built a plugin that we ask you to install, which will collect the required data to measure this phonomenom.

[ Data Collection ]

1

We do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII). To distinguish you between all the users, we generate an ID of 256 bits created once you install the extension. This ID remains stored on the browser until you remove the extension.

We collect interests associated with your Google and Facebook profiles. We collect the interestes using the Ad Preference Managers (APMs) of each platform every 20 minutes. This process is completely automated, but you need to be logged into your Facebook and Google account before install the extensions.

The domains that you visit. We only collect the Fully Qualify Domain Name, and not the full URL.

[ Installation ]

  1. Download the extension thought the button:

  2. Open Chrome and visit the url chrome://extensions/. In case that you are using Edge, visit edge://extensions/.

  3. Once you have visited the url, enable the “Develope Mode”. The button is located in the top left of the window, as you can appreciate in the following image:

  4. Locate the extension, unzipe it and drag the unziped folder (overprofiling) to the browser, as we describe in the gif.

  5. Once you accept the terms of use,the extension will show you a list of interest associated to your Facebook and Google profiles. Please, select how representative the interests are according to your online behaviour. Finally, we encourage you to click in the extension's icon to recieve a new survey about your interests

If you see multiple tabs opening and closing when you visit Facebook, do not worry. Unfortunately, it is the only way we have to collect the explanations about why you received an ad.
